Workshop Description

This is a one day workshop that focuses on technical presentation and technical writing skills of the students. In addition it focuses on making you aware about the internship opportunities and higher studies and help you to get through these.

We will be covering these points in the session:

  1. How to make technical slides?

  2. How to present your ideas?

  3. How to apply for internships at prestigious places like IIT, Google, Amazon, Microsoft?

  4. How to crack an interview?

  5. How to apply for scholarhips?

  6. Discuss with the people who have grabed these opportunities.
