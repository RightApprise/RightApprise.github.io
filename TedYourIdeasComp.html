Ted YourIdeas Competition

Time to capture your story and test your presentation skills.

 

Step 1: Create a video to show your presentation skills. We have given some hints below, but you are open to choose even any other topic.

  • Explain any topic of science, even a small topic that can be covered in 3 minutes  like why leaves are green, how raining system works.
  • Pick any puzzle and solve it.
  • Teach some concepts to kids like a collection of words like “except” and “accept”, How to write an application, How to write an essay.
  • Pick any small concept of programming or software. Like: how to write first code in C, how to define a function in any programming language, How to install any given software

  • Your technical journey like life as a software engineer etc.

 

Step 2: Upload video on Google drive.

 

Step 3:  Fill this form (https://goo.gl/forms/quVVkX5711wKhbHE2) with the link of the video. Give all rights before you share the link.

 

Step 4: We will upload your video online and will mail you the link. Share it with your friends and get it liked.
